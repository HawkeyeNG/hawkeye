Public Docket

When automated checks flag a result, the flag never decides anything — it opens a case. Every case is published here with its full evidence, and verified observers worldwide judge it by answering factual questions. Quorum and a supermajority resolve it, every verdict is on the public record, and the whole docket is anchored to a public transparency log.
