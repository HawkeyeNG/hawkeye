About Hawkeye

Hawkeye is an independent, non-partisan transparency platform that lets ordinary Nigerians document, verify and publish the results announced at their polling units — so the numbers can be checked by anyone.

Why it exists

Elections are won and lost at the polling unit and then at collation. When results can be altered on the way up, trust collapses. Hawkeye puts a network of independent witnesses on the ground: every report is photographed, signed on the observer's own phone, and recorded on a public, tamper-evident ledger. A count is only trusted when independent observers at the same place agree — corroborated by GPS, venue-photo matching and machine-reading of the sheet itself.

What we are — and are not

Hawkeye does not declare winners and its tallies are not official results. It is a public, independent record of what was announced, built to aid scrutiny. The official results of any election are those declared by the Independent National Electoral Commission (INEC). Hawkeye is not affiliated with INEC, any political party, candidate or campaign.
