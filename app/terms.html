What Hawkeye is

Hawkeye lets citizens photograph, sign, and publish the results announced at their polling unit to an independent, tamper-evident public record. Hawkeye is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or acting on behalf of INEC, any government body, or any political party. Official election results are those declared by INEC alone. Hawkeye produces crowd-reported evidence for public scrutiny; it does not, and cannot, declare a winner.

Acceptable use
  • Submit only truthful reports of what you genuinely witnessed at a polling unit. Do not fabricate, alter, or misrepresent results, photos, or locations.
  • One verified phone number is one observer identity. Do not attempt to create multiple identities, evade anti-fraud controls, or file duplicate or automated reports.
  • Do not upload unlawful, defamatory, or harassing content, or media you have no right to share.
  • Do not attempt to disrupt, overload, reverse-engineer, or gain unauthorised access to the service.
  • Never put yourself at risk to gather a report. Follow all applicable laws.
Your content & the public ledger

Reports you submit — counts, photos, and their locations — are published on a public, tamper-evident ledger and, by design, cannot be edited or deleted; that permanence is what makes them trustworthy. By submitting, you grant Hawkeye a non-exclusive, worldwide, royalty-free licence to store, publish, and display that content for election-transparency purposes. You confirm you have the right to submit it. Your phone number is never published — see the Privacy Policy.
