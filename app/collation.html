Report a Collation Result

At a ward (EC8B), LGA (EC8C) or state (EC8D) collation centre? Photograph the announced collation form and enter its totals. Hawkeye automatically checks them against the polling-unit sheets underneath — numbers that shrink on the way up get flagged publicly.
