## Why this matters

Hawkeye's value is trust: citizens rely on the name to know they are looking at
the genuine, tamper-evident record. A fork that *calls itself* Hawkeye could
publish fabricated "results" under our identity. Open code, protected name is
the standard defense — the same model used by Mozilla (Firefox), Signal, and
the Linux Foundation.
